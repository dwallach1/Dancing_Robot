"Dancing Robot" by David Wallach

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
This is your control panel. Make this robot do the robot dance!

press the "h" key for instructions on how to use this program (they will appear directly under this message)

To further interact with this project, you can:
1. use the right arrow key to increase the rotation angle
2. use the left arrow key to decrease the rotation angle
3. press space bar to make his feet appear or disappear
4. drag the hexagonal figure around with your mouse
5. toggle the "Do the robot button" to enable the robot to do the robot dance
6. toggle the "freeze" button to make the animations hault or continue
7. click the canvas to change the color of the background
8. press the "a" key to make a secret shape roam the sceen
9. once the "a" key has been pressed, you can press the "s" key to make it disappear
